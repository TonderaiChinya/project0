But I did not throw away my dreams. Down the line, I enrolled with UNISA (BSc Informatics) through the e-learning platform. Again, I dropped out due to lack of access to resources. From the period of 2011 to 2015 I was working in the tech but did not have the opportunity to advance my studies.

From 2018 to current date, through hard work and sacrifices, I have been enhancing my skills through different online platforms. So as I stand today, I look forward to venture fully into tech industry and improve my skills to become better and also to utilize my current skills in the development of modern tech.
